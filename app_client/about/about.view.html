About Fete

Have you ever been out on a weekend night and wondered if there was a party going on? It's a hassle to ask around and find out. Our site will eliminate the need to ask, and will bring the parties’ locations to you.

Fete, which means celebration, festival or party is a web application that will inform users of upcoming events/parties. Fete will seamlessly integrate our web application with our Android/IOS mobile application to give the user the same experience on both devices.

Fete was created using the MEAN stack ( (M)ondodb, (E)xpress, (A)ngular.js, and (N)ode.js).
